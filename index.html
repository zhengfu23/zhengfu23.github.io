Zheng's Personal Website

Welcome to my personal website. Hope you'll enjoy your visit.

Technology is transforming our everday lives. I would like to transform the way people live in their own homes. IoT (picture from canvs.org), or internet of things, is a network consists of all everyday "things" that we use with embedded electronics in them. Things can communicate with each other, transfer data with or without human interaction. This introduced a whole lot of Artificial intelligence components as well as automation.

This is where I come in. There are a lot of possibilities and existing devices on the market, but they are either ridiculously high priced or very hard to integrate into everyday life.

Imagine your lights turn on without you even thinking of it.

Imagine your enviroment adapts to your habit and even mood.

Imagine your home truly cares and knows about you.

Welcome to the future.
